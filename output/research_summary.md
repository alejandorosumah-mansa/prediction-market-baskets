# Reddit Research Summary: Prediction Market Basket/ETF User Demand

**Research Date:** February 23, 2026
**Researcher:** OpenClaw Subagent
**Client:** Aditis (Prediction Market Structured Products Company)

## Executive Summary

I successfully scraped Reddit across key prediction market communities to identify user demand for basket/ETF-like products in prediction markets. The research uncovered **9 high-quality entries** across multiple subreddits, revealing strong demand for structured products, portfolio management tools, and cross-platform arbitrage capabilities.

## Key Findings

### 1. **Strong Demand for Cross-Platform Arbitrage Tools**
- Multiple users actively seeking tools to exploit price differences between Polymarket and Kalshi
- One user reported making **$1,400/month with $8K capital** using arbitrage strategies
- Users want automated systems rather than manual monitoring

### 2. **Portfolio Management Infrastructure Gaps**
- Users building custom trading bots and AI systems to manage multiple prediction markets simultaneously
- Demand for "ETF-like" infrastructure that can deploy capital across multiple markets/timeframes
- Need for risk management tools and portfolio protection

### 3. **Basket Strategy Interest**
- Users asking about combo bets and multi-market strategies
- Interest in systematic approaches that don't require predicting individual outcomes
- Desire for structured products that reduce individual market risk

2. **Specific Features Requested:**

3. **Target User Segments:**
